The Design Principles

This System is designed for grading purpose for CS765 Data Visualization of UW-Madison students. The grading system use data of homework which including Discussion and Seek and Find two parts. For discussion tasks, students need to provide inforamtion about the homework and reading also the understanding of class material. The discussion task proceeds by finishing required several postings and a limited group of people randomly assigned will have acess to the postings and comments. The seek and find task is aming to present a good or not-that-good visualization from sources. And the critique need to be supplied with that.
