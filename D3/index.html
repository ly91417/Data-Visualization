The Design Principles

This System is designed for grading purpose for CS 765 Data Visualization of UW-Madison students. The grading system use data of homework which including Discussion and Seek and Find two parts. For discussion tasks, students need to provide information about the homework and reading also the understanding of class material. The discussion task proceeds by finishing required several postings and a limited group of people randomly assigned will have access to the postings and comments. The seek and find task is aiming to present a good or visualization with defects. The goal of this program is to build a system visualize the students course data for CS 765 instructor.
