  • For this visualization, the task is to explore the late submissions patterns of each student
  • The x axis is the assignments throught the semester which could be encoding time or sequence of assignments
  • The y axis is the alphabetical ordered students
  • The exists of the circle can be used to encode the late submission information
  • The color of the circle and the numerical value of late hours are chosen to redundantly encode the lateness of submission
  • Label M with red font is utilized to encode the missing submission information
  • One of the petential secondary task could be to find the lateness between groups. In order to do that, we need more information of group assignment for each posting of each student. This could be done with more time and more data filed in the data source.
  • Aggregated information of each student's score need to be provided to compare the lateness effect on the final score.
